What are Leadership Metrics?

Leadership Papers = First Author Papers + Last Author Papers
Leadership Citations = First Author Citations + Last Author Citations

These combined metrics recognize that both first authorship (leading the work) and last/senior authorship (supervising the work) represent leadership roles in academic publishing.

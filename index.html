About the Yousem Metric

The Yousem Metric (YM) is a bibliometric measure that emphasizes leadership roles in academic publishing.

Formula: YM = Weighted Citations + (10 × Weighted Papers)

Weighting:

  • First/Last author positions: 5× weight
  • Middle author positions: 1× weight

This metric rewards researchers who lead projects (first author) or supervise them (last/senior author), rather than just accumulating middle-author credits.

About Leadership Metrics

Leadership Papers = First Author Papers + Last Author Papers

Leadership Citations = First Author Citations + Last Author Citations

Both first and last authorship positions represent leadership in academic medicine — first authors lead the project execution, while last authors typically supervise as senior investigators.

Available Metrics

  • Total Papers – All publications in Scopus
  • Total Citations – Cumulative citation count
  • H-Index – Hirsch index from Scopus
  • First Author Papers/Citations – Publications where listed first
  • Last Author Papers/Citations – Publications where listed last (senior author)
  • Leadership Papers/Citations – Combined first + last author metrics
  • Yousem Metric – Weighted measure emphasizing leadership roles
